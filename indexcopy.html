Internship

ISRO’s Office paid internships allow high school and college-level students to contribute to the agency’s mission to advance science, technology, aeronautics, and space exploration.

Internships offer students an opportunity to gain practical work experience while with mentors who are research scientists, engineers are other professions. The provision of scholarships elevates this initiative, creating a pathway for talented individuals from both groups ISRO contribute significantly to the future of space exploration.

Internship Program offers stipends to participants to support them during their internship period.

Join our ISRO team and gain valuable on-the-job experience, build your resume, and strengthen your career readiness. We offer three sessions annually, so visit our website often for opportunities.
